Venus
/

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty. As the second-brightest natural object in the night sky after the Moon, Venus can cast shadows and, rarely, is visible to the naked eye in broad daylight. The atmosphere of Venus is very hot and thick since it is made up mainly of carbon dioxide, and thick clouds of sulfuric acid completely cover the planet.
